Chairman’s Message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Barishal University. Our mission is to foster innovation, nurture critical thinking, and prepare students to address the challenges of the ever-evolving digital world.

Through quality teaching, advanced research, and strong industry collaboration, we aim to empower our graduates with the knowledge and skills required to excel in both academia and professional practice.

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Barishal University is a hub of innovation, research, and academic excellence. With a strong foundation in both theoretical and applied computer science, we prepare our students to become future leaders in technology, research, and industry.

Our programs are designed to foster critical thinking, hands-on problem-solving, and collaboration. From Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity to Human-Computer Interaction and Cloud Computing, we cover a wide range of cutting-edge areas.
